# Repository Categories

This collection is organized into the following categories:

## Cross-Chain & Interoperability
Focus on moving value and data between chains.

## DeFi & Lending
Lending, borrowing, yield, and perpetuals.

## Stablecoins, Payments & Treasury
Payment rails, settlement, and institutional tools.

## NFTs, Gaming & Rewards
NFT marketplaces and GameFi systems.

## Meme Coins & Token Launches
Fair launch platforms and token creation tools.

## RWA Tokenization & Credentials
Real-world asset tokenization and on-chain identity.

## DEX & L2 Tools
DEXes and tools for comparing L2s.

Each category has starters optimized for the strengths of the respective chains (Base, Arc, Solana, etc.).
